Things25 unattempted yet in prose26 or rhyme27.

Wast present33, and, with mighty wings34 outspread,

And justify the ways44 of God to men45.

Nor the deep tract48 of Hell49--say first what50 cause51

Moved our grand parents52, in that happy state53,

Favoured of Heaven so highly, to fall off

From their Creator, and transgress his will

For one restraint54, lords55 of the World besides.

Th' infernal Serpent; he it was whose guile58,

Stirred up with envy59 and revenge60, deceived

Had cast him out from Heaven, with all his host65
